Unit One Highlight

This is John Nelson's page, and one highlight from Unit one in CSE was making our own app. The first highlight of this class is from Unit one in CSE making our own app. I made a whack-a-mole-like game where you had to tap on a moving sprite to get points and reach the next level. I really enjoyed making this game because of how much freedom we had, and this was my first time where I felt like I knew what I was coding and had a goal in mind, rather than just following directions.

Unit Two Highlight

The second unit we did was the SDV's. This unit was probably my least favorite simply because of how inconsistent the SDV's vision sensor was. Even the smallest change of light would completely throw off the vision sensor, forcing you to reset it which would take at least 5 minutes. However, I really enjoyed the satisfaction of finally getting it to work, especially in the final part of the unit which was making a convoy of the SDV's. Although I did not enjoy developing this project, in my opinion, this was the coolest part of this class.

Unit Three Highlight

The third unit we did was primarily on coding in Python. We were given the task of creating an app that would help the school. Paul and I decided to create an app that could track your progress in the weight room and calculate the percentage of your lift. I felt that I had the best grasp on what we were trying to accomplish in this unit, and the final product was one of my favorites to complete. We also came up with the super cool name, World of Weights, also known as WOW.
